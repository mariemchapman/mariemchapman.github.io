Venue

Both the ceremony and reception will be held at the Torpedo Factory Art Center in historic Old Town, Alexandria, overlooking the Potomac River. Founded in 1974 and housed in a former munitions factory, the Torpedo Factory provides subsidized, publicly-accessible studio space to artists from across the globe, with 71 working artists currently in residence. TFAC is also home to the Alexandria Archaeology Museum and The Art League of Alexandria.

Schedule of Events

6:30PM-7:00PM - Ceremony - Main Hall (ground floor).

7:00PM-8:00PM - Cocktail Hour - Overlook Room (3rd floor). Open bar with passed appetizers.

8:00PM-12:30AM - Dinner & Reception - Main Hall (ground floor). Buffet-style dinner and dessert from local Old Blue BBQ, speeches, cake cutting, and lots of dancing!




Accessibility

The Torpedo Factory is fully accessible via entry ramps on the ground floor of the building and an elevator to the upper levels.
