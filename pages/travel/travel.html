Traveling to Alexandria

Old Town Alexandria is easily accessible by plane, train, and car, with its own dedicated Amtrak station in addition to the nearby Union Station in DC.

The closest Metro station to the venue and hotel is King St Station (Yellow/Blue Line).

The closest airport is Reagan National, which is just a 10-minute drive or two Metro stops away from the center of Old Town. Alternately, Dulles International Airport is a 40-minute drive from Alexandria, and is also accessible by Metro.

If you are driving, parking is available for Westin guests, and there are multiple public lots and ample street parking within easy walking distance of the venue.

Hotel

We have reserved a block of 20 rooms at the Westin Alexandria Old Town for the nights of 10/17 and 10/18, for a reduced rate of $199/night plus tax. These rooms will be held for us until 9/15/25, after which point the Westin can no longer guarantee the discounted rate.

The hotel is 1.3 miles from the Torpedo Factory, and is just two blocks off of King St, the central dining and shopping strip of Old Town.

While the distance to TFAC is walkable, there is also a free trolley that runs the length of King St during the day, as well as excellent Uber/Lyft coverage throughout the area.

If you would like to seek accomodation elsewhere, there are dozens of nearby hotels at varying price points throughout Alexandria and neighboring Crystal City, including several with direct proximity to Reagan National Airport. Please be sure to arrange your own post-reception transportation if you are not staying at the Westin!
